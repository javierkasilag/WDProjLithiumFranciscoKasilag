Berlin

(Greater Germanic Reich)



Berlin serves as the seat of the Nazi regime, being the largest and most populous city in all of the Greater Germanic Reich. It is the central point of the Neue Ordnung, serving as the primary administrative capital of Germany by housing everything from economic districts to the party offices of the NSDAP. A stroll around the city yields the intimidating and brutalistic sight of swastikas, sprawling buildings, propaganda, and statues of political figures in all corners. The German government is carrying out its incredibly ambitious and economically taxing plans to transform Berlin into the "World Capital" Germania, an Aryan metropolis envisioned to rival and exceed the great ancient cities belonging to Egypt, Babylon, Rome, and others.
